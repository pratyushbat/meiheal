Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

At Health Store With meiheal, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book a consultation, or purchase a Health Store plan.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with highly personalized dietary services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number (e.g., WhatsApp), and billing details.
  • Health and Lifestyle Data: Age, weight, height, dietary preferences, food allergies, medical history, and fitness routines provided during consultations or intake forms.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and interactions with our website (via cookies and analytics tools).

3. Confidentiality of Health Information

Your health data is strictly confidential.

The personal and medical information you share to receive customized Health Store plans is used exclusively for that purpose. We will never sell, rent, or publicly share your health metrics, progress photos, or dietary restrictions without your explicit written consent.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To design and deliver your customized Health Store and nutrition plans.
  • To communicate with you regarding your appointments, progress, and plan adjustments via WhatsApp or email.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to facilitate our business, such as payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) and communication platforms (e.g., WhatsApp, Instagram). These third parties only have access to the personal information necessary to perform their specific tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to any inaccurate data, or request the deletion of your data once our services are concluded. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
